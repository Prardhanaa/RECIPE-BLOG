Mango Pickle Recipe
Ingredients:
  • Raw mangoes – 4 cups (chopped)
  • Red chili powder – 1/2 cup
  • Mustard powder – 1/2 cup
  • Salt – 1/2 cup
  • Fenugreek seeds – 1 tbsp (powdered)
  • Garlic – 10 cloves
  • Gingelly oil – 1 cup
Preparation:
  1. Wash and dry raw mangoes completely. Cut into small cubes.
  2. Mix mustard powder, chili powder, salt, and fenugreek powder.
  3. Add mango pieces and garlic, coat well with the spice mix.
  4. Pour gingelly oil, mix thoroughly.
  5. Store in a clean glass jar. Leave for 3-4 days before use.
Gongura Pickle Recipe
Ingredients:
  • Gongura leaves – 4 cups
  • Dry red chilies – 12
  • Garlic – 10 cloves
  • Mustard seeds – 1 tsp
  • Fenugreek seeds – 1/2 tsp
  • Salt – as needed
  • Oil – 1/2 cup
Preparation:
  1. Wash gongura leaves and dry them well. Fry until soft and tangy.
  2. Dry roast red chilies, mustard, and fenugreek, then powder them.
  3. Grind gongura leaves with garlic and spice powder.
  4. Heat oil, add the paste, and fry for 5-6 minutes.
  5. Cool and store in an airtight jar.
Lemon Pickle Recipe
Ingredients:
  • Lemons – 10 (medium size)
  • Salt – 1/2 cup
  • Red chili powder – 1/4 cup
  • Turmeric – 1 tsp
  • Methi powder – 1 tsp
  • Oil – 1/2 cup
Preparation:
  1. Wash and dry lemons completely. Cut into small pieces.
  2. Add salt and turmeric, mix and keep in a jar for 3 days.
  3. After 3 days, add chili powder and methi powder.
  4. Heat oil, cool it, and pour over pickle.
  5. Mix well and store in a dry glass jar.
