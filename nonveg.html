Idli with Chicken / Mutton
Ingredients:
  • 4-6 Idlis
  • 200g Chicken or Mutton pieces
  • Onion, Tomato, Garlic, Ginger
  • Spices: Turmeric, Chili powder, Garam masala, Salt
  • Oil for cooking
Preparation:
  1. Cook chicken/mutton with spices, onion, tomato, garlic, and ginger.
  2. Steam idlis until soft and fluffy.
  3. Serve hot idlis with prepared curry and raita/perugu pacchadi.

Color Rice with Non-Veg Curries
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups steamed rice
  • 200g Chicken or Mutton curry
  • 150g Prawns or Crab curry (optional)
  • Spices: Chili powder, Turmeric, Coriander, Garam masala, Salt
  • Oil for cooking
Preparation:
  1. Prepare individual non-veg curries with spices.

  • 200g fish pieces (preferably freshwater fish)
  • Onion, Tomato, Garlic, Ginger
  • Spices: Tamarind, Chili powder, Turmeric, Coriander, Salt
  • Oil for cooking
Preparation:

  2. Cook steamed rice until fluffy.
  3. Serve fish curry hot with rice.
Biryani
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 200g Chicken / Mutton / Fish / Egg / Prawns
  • Onion, Tomato, Garlic, Ginger
  • Spices: Garam masala, Turmeric, Chili powder, Coriander, Bay leaves, Salt
  • Yogurt, Oil, Ghee
Preparation:
  1. Marinate meat or fish with spices and yogurt for 30-60 mins.
  2. Fry onions, prepare curry base, and layer partially cooked rice.
  3. Cook on low flame for 20-25 mins until biryani is done.
  4. Serve hot with raita or salad.
