Ribbon Pakodi
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/4 cup gram flour (besan)
  • Red chili powder, carom seeds
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for deep frying
Preparation:
  1. Mix rice flour, gram flour, and spices with water to form a thick batter.
  2. Heat oil in a deep pan.
  3. Using a ribbon pakodi press, shape batter into hot oil.
  4. Deep fry until crispy. Drain and serve hot.
Karam Poosa
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup maida (all-purpose flour)
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Oil for deep frying
Preparation:
  1. Mix flour, sugar, and chili powder into dough with water.
  2. Shape into twists or coils.
  3. Deep fry until golden and crispy.
  4. Serve warm.
Chakodilu
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/4 cup urad dal flour
  • Salt and chili powder
  • Oil for deep frying
Preparation:
  1. Mix flours, salt, and chili powder with water.
  2. Shape using chakodilu mould.
  3. Deep fry until golden.
Nippatlu
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup rice flour
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
Preparation:
  1. Mix rice flour and salt with water to make stiff dough.
  2. Roll thin discs and shape.
  3. Deep fry until crisp.
