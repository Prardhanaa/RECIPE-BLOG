Tomato Soup
Ingredients:
  • 4 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp butter (optional)
Preparation:
  1. Heat butter in a pan, saut√© onions and garlic until soft.
  2. Add tomatoes and cook until mushy.
  3. Add vegetable broth, simmer 10 minutes.
  4. Blend until smooth, season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve hot with a garnish of herbs.
Pepper Rasam
Ingredients:
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1/2 cup tamarind extract
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Curry leaves
Preparation:
  1. Crush peppercorns and cook with tamarind water.
  2. Add tomato and boil 5 minutes.
  3. Add salt and curry leaves.
  4. Serve hot as a digestive soup.
Lemon Rasam
Ingredients:
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 2 cups tamarind water
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Curry leaves
Preparation:
  1. Boil tamarind water with tomato and spices.
  2. Add lemon juice and salt.
  3. Garnish with curry leaves and serve hot.
Vegetable Clear Soup
Ingredients:
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1 capsicum, sliced
  • Beans, chopped
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt & pepper to taste
Preparation:
  1. Boil vegetables in broth 10 minutes.
  2. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Serve hot.
Spinach Soup
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups spinach leaves
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp cream (optional)
Preparation:
  1. Saut√© onion and garlic, add spinach and broth.
  2. Simmer 10 minutes and blend until smooth.
  3. Add cream if desired, season, and serve hot.
Chicken Soup
Ingredients:
  • 200g chicken pieces
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • Salt & pepper to taste
Preparation:
  1. Boil chicken and vegetables in broth for 20 minutes.
  2. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Serve hot.
Mutton Soup
Ingredients:
  • 200g mutton pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 2 cups water
  • Spices & salt to taste
Preparation:
  1. Cook mutton slowly with onion, tomato, and spices 40 minutes.
  2. Serve hot as a hearty soup.
Egg Drop Soup
Ingredients:
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tsp cornstarch (optional)
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Chopped spring onions
Preparation:
  1. Heat broth and bring to a gentle boil.
  2. Slowly pour beaten eggs while stirring to form ribbons.
  3. Add cornstarch for thickness (optional), season, and garnish with spring onions.
  4. Serve hot.
Prawn Soup
Ingredients:
  • 200g prawns, cleaned
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 2 cups water or seafood stock
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt & pepper to taste
Preparation:
  1. Saut√© onion, tomato, and ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Add prawns and cook 5 minutes.
  3. Add stock, simmer 10 minutes, season, and serve hot.
Fish Soup
Ingredients:
  • 200g fish fillets
  • 2 cups water or fish stock
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp chopped coriander
  • Salt & pepper to taste
Preparation:
  1. Boil water with onion and ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Add fish fillets, simmer 10 minutes.
  3. Season with salt, pepper, garnish with coriander, and serve hot.
Crab Soup
Ingredients:
  • 200g crab meat
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 2 cups water or seafood stock
  • Spices & salt to taste
Preparation:
  1. Saut√© onion and tomato, add crab meat and spices.
  2. Add stock, simmer 15 minutes.
  3. Season and serve hot.
Paya Soup
Ingredients:
  • 2-3 goat trotters, cleaned
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2-3 cups water
  • Spices & salt to taste
Preparation:
  1. Boil trotters with onion, garlic, and spices for 2-3 hours until tender.
  2. Strain, season, and serve hot as a hearty soup.
