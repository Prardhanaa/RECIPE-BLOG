Payasam
Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup rice or vermicelli
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • Cardamom, cashews, raisins
Preparation:
  1. Boil milk in a pan, add rice/vermicelli and cook until soft.
  2. Add sugar, cardamom, and simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  3. Fry cashews and raisins in ghee and add to payasam.
  4. Serve hot or chilled.
Bobatlu
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup chana dal
  • 1/2 cup jaggery or sugar
  • 1 cup wheat flour
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Ghee for cooking
Preparation:
  1. Cook dal until soft and mash.
  2. Add jaggery and cardamom to make sweet filling.
  3. Prepare soft dough from wheat flour, roll discs, place filling, fold and seal.
  4. Cook on hot griddle with ghee until golden both sides.
Sunnundalu
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup roasted gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 cup jaggery powder
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Ghee for binding
Preparation:
  1. Mix roasted gram flour and jaggery powder.
  2. Add cardamom and little ghee to bind into small balls.
  3. Shape into round laddus and store.
