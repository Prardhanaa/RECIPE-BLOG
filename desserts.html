Gulab Jamoon
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1/2 cup milk (adjust consistency)
  • Sugar syrup
Preparation:
  1. Mix milk powder, flour, and ghee.
  2. Add milk slowly to form soft dough.
  3. Shape into small balls and deep fry on low heat.
  4. Soak fried balls in warm sugar syrup and serve.
Rasmalai
Ingredients:
  • 1 liter milk
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • Cardamom powder
Preparation:
  1. Boil milk and add lemon juice to curdle it.
  2. Drain curds and shape into small discs.
  3. Boil sugar syrup and cook discs until soft.
  4. Serve chilled with thickened milk and cardamom.
Kheer / Phirni
Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 1 liter milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • Cardamom powder and nuts
Preparation:
  1. Wash rice and cook in milk until soft.
  2. Add sugar and cardamom.
  3. Garnish with nuts and serve warm or chilled.
Carrot Halwa
Ingredients:
  • 500g carrots, grated
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • Nuts and cardamom
Preparation:
  1. Cook grated carrots in milk until soft.
  2. Add ghee, sugar, and cook until it thickens.
  3. Garnish with nuts and cardamom.
  4. Serve warm.
Fruit Custard
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp custard powder
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • Mixed fruits (apple, banana, grapes, etc.)
Preparation:
  1. Dissolve custard powder in a little milk.
  2. Boil remaining milk with sugar, then add custard mixture.
  3. Cook until thickened.
  4. Add chopped fruits and serve chilled.
Falooda
Ingredients:
  • Falooda sev / vermicelli
  • Rose syrup
  • Milk
  • Basil seeds
  • Ice cream (optional)
Preparation:
  1. Soak basil seeds in water until they swell.
  2. Cook falooda sev as per instructions.
  3. Layer milk, rose syrup, sev, and basil seeds in a glass.
  4. Top with ice cream if desired and serve chilled.
